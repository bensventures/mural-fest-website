Mural Fest 2025

The national “Collage” festival
14, 15, 16 November 2025 in Barcelona


Ticketing available soon!

We’re back with a new edition of the Mural Fest in Apocapoc, Barcelona!

A new opportunity to participate in Collage type worskhops, learn about many environmental topics and understand how to take action to reduce our environmental footprint on the planet. Climate, biodiversity, textile, digital, desirable future, water, ocean, circular economy, living soils…

And as with all festivals, there will be music, party, and other movement and artistic activities!

3 days to enjoy, learn, connect with the Sustainability ecosystem in Barcelona and beyond!

Save the dates: 14, 15 and 16 November! Make sure you book your ticket!

Activities

The Climate Fresk

A collaborative game to raise awareness about climate change and find collective solutions to meet the challenges

New Narrarives Collage

Discover through a collaborative card game the influence of stories on our brains and our behaviour.

The Biodiversity Collage

The Biodiversity Collage is a fun and cooperative workshop that aims to raise awareness about the importance of biodiversity and the causes and consequences of its erosion.

The Digital Collage

The Digital Collage is a fun and collaborative half-day workshop with a similar educational method as the Climate Fresk. The workshop also aims to lay down the key solutions to build a more sustainable digital sector. It then encourages participants to discuss on the topic for fruitful debate.

The ADAptation to Climate Change workshop

Both mitigation and adaptation strategies are needed to face climate change. The ADAptation to Climate Change workshop focuses on adaptation. It proposes a methodology to understand the components of a risk and provides the keys to collectively choose the best possible options and avoid maladaptation.

Ocean Collage

Discover the impact on our planet, tackle pressing issues, and explore actionable solutions in a session limited to only 10 spots. This ensures a personalized experience where every question and idea can shine.

The glorious 2030

A workshop about traveling to the glorious 2030's to get out of distressing dystopias and dare to dream of a better world. For 2.5 hours, we will dive into a glorious 2030 of profound transformations in our society.

Inventing Low Carbon Roads

A participatory workshop to identify the individual and collective levers for action to combat climate change, the erosion of biodiversity and pressure on resources.

2 Tons

In 3 hours and in teams, project yourself to 2050, discover the individual and collective levers of the low-carbon transition, and identify the role you want to play in it!

Textiles UnTanggled

Co-created by Green Donut volunteers to raise awareness of the environmental and social impacts of the textile industry, its aim is to stimulate thought and debate on the actions that individuals and organisations can take to help the sector reduce its impact.

An introduction to creating psychological safety

Learn to cultivate a fearless work environment through psychological safety, enhancing communication, trust, and innovation.

Sustainable Pathways

An interactive, team-based experience that inspires sustainable change, from boardroom to shop floor.

The Conscious Evolution Collage

The Conscious Evolution Collage workshop brings to life how all these global issues are interconnected and explores the deeper psychological roots that are causing these issues. Join us for a 3-hour card game based around collective understanding and knowledge sharing.

Circular Economy Collage

Join our transformative Circular Economy Collage workshop! Dive into the pressing issues of resource consumption, waste generation, and their impacts on our planet.

Agri'Alim workshop

Playful and collaborative workshop to understand the causes and solutions to food waste, and to raise public awareness in the fight to reduce it.

Make It Awkward

This collaborative and interactive workshop will help you to expand your repertoire of replies and refine your “answer to biased comments” technique. Make it Awkward workshop has been created by Siara Isaac (from CAPE, EPFL) and Vivek Ramachandran (EDRS) to respond more effectively when we see biased comments or behaviors.

About ApocApoc

Apocapoc Nest City Lab is a Culture Lab promoting regenerative habits and sustainable practices. The Nest city lab facilitates a lifestyle that reduces our ecological footprint on a daily basis and encourages us to have a positive impact on the world.

Apocapoc Nest City Lab supports Mural Fest for the second year already and welcomes the community of environmental workshop facilitators in Barcelona and around all along the year.

About Climate Fresk

The Climate Fresk is a scientific, collaborative and creative workshop, designed to raise awareness, in a playful way, about climate change.

With rigor and pedagogy, this game allows us to discover in a short time how the climate system works, as well as the causes and consequences of its imbalance.

By participating in the workshop, participants become aware of the complexity of climate change. They are given an overview of this vast problem and are provided with the keys to understanding how to act effectively.

In addition to providing scientific knowledge, the Climate Fresk stimulates the creativity and human skills of the participants.

The Climate Fresk is backed up by an association created in 2018 which helps a community of over 84,000 facilitators who supported more than 1.8 millions participants in the whole world.

It is the second consecutive year the association Climate Fresk is helping the Spanish community to organise the Mural Fest.

About the organizers

Behind this event are volunteer environmental and social workshop facilitators. We are passionate about transmitting knowledge on those topics. We are trained and certified for each of the workshops we offer. Our community organise workshops in all of Spain and online to bring understanding on environmental and social challenges to the most people possible, having fun and empowering them to take action to create a more just and sustainable world.
