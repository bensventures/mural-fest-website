Mural Fest 2025

The festival of collaborative environmental and social awareness workshops
14, 15, 16 November 2025 in Barcelona


Book your ticket →

We’re back with a new edition of the Mural Fest in Apocapoc, Barcelona!

A new opportunity to participate in collage-type worskhops, learn about many environmental topics and understand how to take action to reduce our environmental footprint on the planet. Climate, biodiversity, textile, digital, desirable future, water, ocean, circular economy, living soils…

And as with all festivals, there will be music, party, and other movement and artistic activities!

3 days to enjoy, learn, connect with the Sustainability ecosystem in Barcelona and beyond.

Save the dates: 14, 15 and 16 November! Make sure you book your ticket!

An event in association with AlterCOP

This year, the Mural Fest will coincide with the COP30 and joins the international initiative AlterCOP. It is a network of alternative events that will occur simultaneously in 10 countries, with the aim to keep corporations and the public informed of COP proceedings and outcomes, facilitate knowledge sharing on corporate sustainability, foster collaboration among various actors and inspire action.

In 2024, AlterCOP 29 in Singapore brought together over 2,300 participants and 250 speakers across 130 sessions. This year, the AlterCOP Global Core Team is partnering with the 3rd edition of the MuralFest , thus extending AlterCOP's vision to Spain to empower local sustainability actions and partnerships.

The Round Table

Del pacto al activismo: ¿cómo enfrentamos la crisis climática a través de las herramientas políticas?

We will open this festival on Friday 14th with a round table bringing together Zarina Kulaeva, researcher in democratic governance in climate change, Tomàs Molina, science communicator, and Maria Serra, cimate activist. We will explore different ways of addressing the climate emergency — from institutional and international agreements to grassroots mobilization and activism. Can we transform the system from within, or must we push it from the outside?

Activities

The Climate Fresk

A collaborative game to raise awareness about climate change and find collective solutions to meet the challenges

New Narratives Collage

Discover through a collaborative card game the influence of stories on our brains and our behaviour.

The Biodiversity Collage

The Biodiversity Collage is a fun and cooperative workshop that aims to raise awareness about the importance of biodiversity and the causes and consequences of its erosion.

The Digital Collage

The Digital Collage is a fun and collaborative half-day workshop with a similar educational method as the Climate Fresk. The workshop also aims to lay down the key solutions to build a more sustainable digital sector. It then encourages participants to discuss on the topic for fruitful debate.

The ADAptation to Climate Change workshop

Both mitigation and adaptation strategies are needed to face climate change. The ADAptation to Climate Change workshop focuses on adaptation. It proposes a methodology to understand the components of a risk and provides the keys to collectively choose the best possible options and avoid maladaptation.

Ocean Collage

The Ocean Collage workshop raises awareness about the issues that weigh upon the ocean (fishing, sea industry, climate change, plastic and chemical pollution) and the impact of our activities on this ecosystem, and gives keys for moving into action.

Planetary Boundaries Fresco

Find out what makes our planet habitable! In 3 hours as a team and in a fun way, discover all the processes compromising the habitability of our planet and adopt a systemic vision to limit your impact!

The Glorious 2030

A workshop about traveling to the glorious 2030's to get out of distressing dystopias and dare to dream of a better world. For 2.5 hours, we will dive into a glorious 2030 of profound transformations in our society.

2 Tons

In 3 hours and in teams, project yourself to 2050, discover the individual and collective levers of the low-carbon transition, and identify the role you want to play in it!

Textiles UnTanggled

Co-created by Green Donut volunteers to raise awareness of the environmental and social impacts of the textile industry, its aim is to stimulate thought and debate on the actions that individuals and organisations can take to help the sector reduce its impact.

An introduction to creating psychological safety

Learn to cultivate a fearless work environment through psychological safety, enhancing communication, trust, and innovation.

Sustainable Pathways

An interactive, team-based experience that inspires sustainable change, from boardroom to shop floor.

The Conscious Evolution Collage

The Conscious Evolution Collage workshop brings to life how all these global issues are interconnected and explores the deeper psychological roots that are causing these issues. Join us for a 3-hour card game based around collective understanding and knowledge sharing.

Circular Economy Collage

Join our transformative Circular Economy Collage workshop! Dive into the pressing issues of resource consumption, waste generation, and their impacts on our planet.

Agri'Alim workshop

Playful and collaborative workshop to understand the causes and solutions to food waste, and to raise public awareness in the fight to reduce it.

Make It Awkward

This collaborative and interactive workshop will help you to expand your repertoire of replies and refine your “answer to biased comments” technique. Make it Awkward workshop has been created by Siara Isaac (from CAPE, EPFL) and Vivek Ramachandran (EDRS) to respond more effectively when we see biased comments or behaviors.

The Finance Collage

The mission of the Finance Collage is to raise awareness of the role of finance in the socio-ecological transition and to get people thinking about the levers for action, on their own scale and whatever their initial level of knowledge, and on a collective scale.

The Living Soils Workshop

It is very recently that the fundamental role of soil in the balance of ecosystems has been highlighted. It is the living interface between plants and minerals, home to a quarter of known biodiversity and harbors life which is responsible for multiple vital functions. Ready to take a dive?

The Water Puzzle Workshop

A participative and collaborative workshop about water resources. Thanks to a set of 57 cards, discover the challenges related to water, its availability, its uses, and how it is affected by human activities and climate change.

Community Boost

Discover your unique community builder profile and explore the 5 stage-framework that transforms scattered groups into engaged communities.
This working is designed for community leaders mobilizing around sustainability issues.
It will give you practical tools and methods that you can immediately apply to boost engagement and develop your authentic style as a community builder.

Taller Cuentagotas

With Cuentagotas, you will understand the water cycle and the alterations caused by climate change, envision yourself in a territory affected by water stress, and explore the different possible solutions and their consequences.

Consentement : Ma Liberté, Nos Limites (French)

This interactive workshop will give you concrete tools to understand and practice informed, enthusiastic, and revocable consent in all your relationships.

No more grey areas — together we’ll explore how consent builds healthy and respectful connections. A workshop to assert yourself, listen to others, and feel truly free.

For children

The event will feature a children's area where kids can have fun under the responsibility of their parents. We're also offering workshops for families and children.

When Objects Come to Life

Family workshop – from 6 years old

What if... we rescue objects that might end up in the trash to create a puppet? Come and create, together with others, your own recycled puppet.

Designing Our Sustainable Lives

Family workshop – from 9 years old

A fun and educational 2-hour workshop designed to help children aged 9 and up design desirable low-carbon lifestyles without making them feel guilty, while identifying necessary, effective, and inspiring actions.

About ApocApoc

Apocapoc Nest City Lab is a Culture Lab promoting regenerative habits and sustainable practices. The Nest city lab facilitates a lifestyle that reduces our ecological footprint on a daily basis and encourages us to have a positive impact on the world.

Apocapoc Nest City Lab supports Mural Fest for the third year already and welcomes the community of environmental workshop facilitators in Barcelona and around all along the year.

About Climate Fresk

The Climate Fresk is a scientific, collaborative and creative workshop, designed to raise awareness, in a playful way, about climate change.

With rigor and pedagogy, this game allows us to discover in a short time how the climate system works, as well as the causes and consequences of its imbalance.

By participating in the workshop, participants become aware of the complexity of climate change. They are given an overview of this vast problem and are provided with the keys to understanding how to act effectively.

In addition to providing scientific knowledge, the Climate Fresk stimulates the creativity and human skills of the participants.

The Climate Fresk is backed up by an association created in 2018 which helps a community of over 99,000 facilitators who supported more than 2.2 million participants in the whole world.

It is the third consecutive year the association Climate Fresk is helping the Spanish community to organise the Mural Fest.

About the organizers
